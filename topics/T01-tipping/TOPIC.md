## Motivation & hypotheses

A deliberately easy, open-ended topic so newcomers have somewhere to put a first
round. Card tips on NYC taxi rides vary — by time of day, by trip length, by pickup
zone, by whether it's an airport run. **Central question:** which of these actually
moves the tip rate, and which are just noise? Any one of them is a fine round.

There is no paper at stake here — the falsifier is trivially "nothing correlates,"
which won't happen. The point is the *loop*, not the finding. Bring a hypothesis,
write the code, let the robot run it.

## Target

None — this is the sandbox. The "target" is that you complete one round end to end.
